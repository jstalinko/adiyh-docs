# Use Custom Landing Page

- [ENGLISH](#english)
- [INDONESIAN](#indonesian)

---

## English

## How to Apply Your Custom Landing Page

After you have created and saved a custom design in the Landing Page Builder, you can apply it to your campaigns.

### Steps to Use

1. Navigate to **ADIYH → Campaign**.
2. Click **Add New Campaign** or **Edit** an existing one.
3. Scroll down to the **Themes** section.
4. Select the Filter dropdown on the top right of the Themes section and choose **LP Builder**.
5. You will see the list of landing pages you created in the Builder.
6. Select your desired custom landing page name.
7. **Save/Update** your campaign.

## Indonesian

## Cara Menggunakan Landing Page Custom

Setelah Anda membuat dan menyimpan desain khusus di Landing Page Builder, Anda dapat menerapkannya pada campaign Anda.

### Langkah-langkah Penggunaan

1. Buka menu **ADIYH → Campaign**.
2. Klik **Add New Campaign** atau **Edit** campaign yang sudah ada.
3. Gulir ke bawah ke bagian **Themes**.
4. Pilih filter dropdown di pojok kanan atas bagian Themes dan pilih **LP Builder**.
5. Anda akan melihat daftar nama landing page yang telah Anda buat di Builder.
6. Pilih nama landing page custom yang Anda inginkan.
7. **Simpan/Update** campaign Anda.
