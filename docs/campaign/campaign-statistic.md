## English

## Campaign Statistics Overview

You can view detailed, real-time traffic statistics for your campaigns.  
**Note:** Campaign statistics are only available if the **"Visitor Logs"** setting is enabled in your campaign configuration.

### How to Access Statistics

1. Go to **ADIYH â†’ Campaign**.
2. Locate your campaign in the list.
3. In the **Action** column (left side), click the **Chart Up Icon** ðŸ“ˆ.
4. You will be redirected to the detailed statistics dashboard.

---

### Statistic Description

#### 1. Stats Overview (Clicks/Visits)

This section shows a summary of traffic actions:

- **Allow**: Number of valid visitors allowed to access the campaign.
- **Block**: Number of visitors blocked (Bot, VPN, unwanted Country/Browser).
- **Error**: Number of errors encountered (if any).
- **Total**: Total number of hits/visits processed.

#### 2. Charts & Distribution

Visual representations of your traffic data (Line & Pie Charts):

- **Countries**: Breakdown of visitors by country.
- **ISP**: Internet Service Provider distribution.
- **Distribution**: Percentage split between Allowed, Blocked, etc.
- **Theme Usage**: Shows which themes were displayed most often. (Very useful if you are using random themes to see which one performs best).

#### 3. Table Logs

A detailed table showing individual visitor logs:

- **Type**: Status of the visit (`Allow` or `Block`).
- **ISP**: Internet Service Provider of the visitor.
- **IP**: Visitor's IP Address.
- **Country**: Visitor's Country.
- **Referer**: The source URL where the visitor came from.
- **OS**: Operating System used.
- **Browser**: Browser used.
- **Date**: Timestamp of the visit.

### Penjelasan Statistik

#### 1. Stats Overview (Klik/Kunjungan)

Bagian ini menampilkan ringkasan aksi traffic:

- **Allow**: Jumlah pengunjung valid yang diizinkan masuk.
- **Block**: Jumlah pengunjung yang diblokir (Bot, VPN, Negara/Browser yang tidak sesuai).
- **Error**: Jumlah error yang terjadi (jika ada).
- **Total**: Total keseluruhan hits/kunjungan yang diproses.

#### 2. Charts & Distribusi

Visualisasi data traffic Anda (Grafik Garis & Pie):

- **Countries**: Sebaran pengunjung berdasarkan negara.
- **ISP**: Distribusi penyedia layanan internet (ISP).
- **Distribution**: Persentase pembagian antara Allowed, Blocked, dll.
- **Theme Usage**: Menampilkan tema mana yang paling sering muncul. (Sangat berguna jika Anda menggunakan fitur tema acak/random themes).

#### 3. Table Logs

Tabel detail yang menampilkan log per pengunjung:

- **Type**: Status kunjungan (`Allow` atau `Block`).
- **ISP**: Provider internet pengunjung.
- **IP**: Alamat IP pengunjung.
- **Country**: Negara pengunjung.
- **Referer**: URL sumber kedatangan pengunjung.
- **OS**: Sistem Operasi yang digunakan.
- **Browser**: Browser yang digunakan.
- **Date**: Kapan kunjungan terjadi.
